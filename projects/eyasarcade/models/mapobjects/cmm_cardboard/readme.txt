CMM_CARDBOARD
===================================================

A set of cardboard boxes with random flaps,
including a fully closed box.

The texture supplied for all models is quite large
1024x1024, you should reduce the size of this
to suit your needs.

Feel free to modify the texture or models to
create other models.

Use these models in your urban terror maps but
please credit CMM forum and wiki

www.custommapmakers.org

Unzip the contents of this zip file to your

mapping/models/cmm_cardboard/ folder

contact 

john@custommapmakers.org
